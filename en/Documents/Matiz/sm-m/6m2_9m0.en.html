MAINTENANCE AND REPAIR

On-Vehicle Service




Roof Rack

Removal Procedure

  1. Remove the front roof rack cover and nuts.
  2. Remove the roof rack from the roof.



Installation Procedure

  1. Install the roof rack with the nuts.

    2. Tighten
    Tighten the roof rack nuts to 7 N•m (62 lb-in).




Roof Molding

Removal Procedure

  1. Remove the roof molding from the plastic clips.



Installation Procedure

  1. Press the roof molding onto the plastic clips.



Rear Spoiler

Removal Procedure

  1. Open the tailgate
  2. Remove the rear spoiler from the tailgate.



  3. Remove the spoiler seals.



Installation Procedure

  1. Install the rear spoiler on the tailgate with the nuts.

    2. Tighten
    Tighten the rear spoiler nuts to 4 N•m (35.5 lb-in).

  2. Install the spoiler seals.



Tailgate Handle

Removal Procedure

  1. Disconnect the negative battery cable.
  2. Open the tailgate.
  3. Remove the tailgate trim.
  4. Remove the tailgate handle nuts.
  5. Remove the license plate lamps. Refer to Section 9B, Lighting Systems.



Installation Procedure

  1. Install the tailgate handle with the nuts.

    2. Tighten
    Tighten the tailgate handle nuts to 5 N•m (44 lb-in).

  2. Install the license plate lamps. Refer to Section 9B, Lighting Systems.
  3. Install the tailgate trim.
  4. Connect the negative battery cable.

GENERAL DESCRIPTION AND SYSTEM OPERATION

Emblems and Lettering

The emblems and lettering on the vehicle are attached by adhesive. The company emblem appears on the hood. The lettering, which appears in several places on the body of the vehicle, features the model, the grade, and the company name.

Mud Guards

Front and rear mud guards are optional equipment on all models. Mud guards will help prevent an excessive buildup of mud on the body.
